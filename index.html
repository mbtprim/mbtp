About MBTP

Founded in 2015 by Abdel Kader Cheiguer, MBTP is a leading Mauritanian company specializing in Building and Public Works. With extensive experience across local projects, we have established ourselves as a reliable and versatile partner serving public, private, and international clients.

Our commitment to excellence and innovation has made us a trusted name in Mauritania's infrastructure development sector.
